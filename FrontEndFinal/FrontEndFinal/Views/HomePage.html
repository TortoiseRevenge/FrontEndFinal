About us

Berkshire Hathaway Inc is an American multinational conglomerate holding company headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska, United States. The company wholly owns several companies such as GEICO, Duracell, and Dairy Queen. Berkshire Hathaway also owns significant shares in companies such as Kraft Heinz, Coca-Cola, and Apple. The company is known for its control and leadership by Warren Buffett, who serves as chairman and chief executive, and by Charlie Munger, one of the company's vice-chairmen.
